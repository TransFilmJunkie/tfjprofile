Goodbye for Now, React

Entry Date: January 21st, 2024

      ╫

There is a major difference between what works in the sterile environment of a class setting, and what works in the real world. In light of this fact, and the fact that React and Github do not get along very well, I have decided to strip out all of the React elements from this profile page. This ensures that the page will behave nicely with Github's Pages feature, and, more importantly, means that this page can be actually released into the real world to serve as a genuine profile page. So, with that in mind, Hello World, I'm coming out onto the internet.

On the Subject of React

Entry Date: December 3rd, 2023

      ╫

Over the past six weeks, one of my major focuses has been on the subject of the Javascript library React. I admit that, at first, I thought that the entire thing was an overcomplication of the basic functions of Javascript. Well dear readers, I have come to the conclusion that I was completely wrong. I have found it to be extremely intuitive and easy-to-use. I have put it to good use in setting up this cheeky portfolio page, and feel that I would be able to make good use of it in the future.
